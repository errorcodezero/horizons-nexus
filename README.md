# Horizons Nexus Project

Idea: You're a bomber pigeon who's goal is to win the war against the evil polluting humans. Your goal? Bomb cities along the world to stop the humans in their tracks. General Greg has chosen you specifically as the greatest flock fighter in the ranks.

Here's your targets:
- Operation Nexus: Bomb San Francisco, United States
- Operation Equinox: Bomb Cairo, Egypt
- Operation Europa: Bomb Berlin, Germany
- Operation Crux: Bomb Sydney, Australia
- Operation Arcana: Bomb Singapore, Singapore
- Operation Polaris: Bomb Toronto, Canada
- Bonus: Operation Sol: Bomb Bogotá, Colombia
